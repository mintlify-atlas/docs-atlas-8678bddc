## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Problem Sets">
    Navigate to `/practice` to find curated sets like NumPy Fundamentals or Neural Networks. Each set contains problems ordered by difficulty.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write Your Solution">
    Open a problem to get a description (with math rendered via KaTeX), a Python template, and a Monaco editor. Write your implementation in the browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run & Submit">
    Hit **Run** to test your code instantly against sample tests. When you're confident, hit **Submit** — your solution is queued, executed in an isolated container, and results are stored permanently.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track Your Progress">
    Check your **Profile** for a submission heatmap and difficulty breakdown. Compete with others on the **Leaderboard** ranked by unique problems solved.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tech Stack

Buildml is built on the [T3 Stack](https://create.t3.gg/) — a production-ready combination of Next.js, TypeScript, Prisma, tRPC, and NextAuth.js — deployed on Vercel with PostgreSQL and Upstash for persistence and queuing.
